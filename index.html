My name is Florence Manglani. I am a self-taught multi-media artist. I live in a beautiful neighborhood in Brooklyn, New York, surrounded by old Victorian style homes and great neighbors. After many years of concentrating on motherhood and a career as a bilingual School Psychologist, I have returned to creating and crafting. I love working with watercolors and pastels but also work with oils and acrylics. I love nature, so I focus on botanicals and landscapes Using different materials, I have created mix-media collages, greeting cards, bookmarks. My first solo art show was at Brooklyn College. My work has also been exhibited at multiple branches of the Brooklyn Public library. I hope you find something on my website that catches your fancy!
